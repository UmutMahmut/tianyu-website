慕光天文台

图：慕光天文台

慕光天文台由李政道研究所青年学者冯发波负责建设，于 2022 年启动、2023 年建成， 主要服务于教学实践、学生科研训练和科普展示。作为李政道研究所楼顶观测平台的重要组成部分， 慕光天文台为本地观测、设备教学和天文教育活动提供平台支撑。

平台概况

慕光天文台兼具教学、实践与展示功能。依托李政道研究所楼顶平台，它既可支持学生开展基础观测训练、 仪器使用学习和初步科研实践，也可用于天文科普与参观展示，是研究所本地天文观测与教育展示的重要载体。

主要设备

  • 35 cm 口径 Planewave CDK14 望远镜
  • QHY600PH 等天文相机设备
  • 若干小型望远镜，用于教学与科普展示

使用场景

慕光天文台适用于教学演示、学生科研训练、设备操作实践和天文科普等场景。 对于学生，它是进入观测学习与科研实践的重要本地平台；对于公众与来访者， 它也是展示研究所天文工作的窗口之一。

Muguang Observatory

Fig. Muguang Observatory

Muguang Observatory was established under the direction of Dr. Feng Fabao at the Tsung-Dao Lee Institute. Construction began in 2022 and was completed in 2023. It primarily serves teaching practice, student research training, and public outreach. As an important part of the TDLI rooftop observing platform, it supports local observing, instrument training, and astronomy education.

Overview

Muguang Observatory integrates teaching, hands-on practice, and public display. Based on the rooftop platform at TDLI, it supports students in observational training, instrument familiarization, and early-stage research practice, while also serving outreach and visiting activities. It is an important local facility for astronomy observation and education at the institute.

Main Instrumentation

  • 35 cm Planewave CDK14 telescope
  • QHY600PH and other astronomical imaging devices
  • Several small telescopes for teaching and outreach

Use Scenarios

Muguang Observatory is suited for teaching demonstrations, student research training, hands-on instrument practice, and astronomy outreach. For students, it provides a local entry point to observational learning and research practice; for visitors and public-facing events, it serves as one of the institute’s windows for presenting its astronomy-related work.
