源启天文台

图：源启天文台

源启天文台于 2024 年 12 月在李政道研究所正式启用， 是研究所楼顶观测平台的重要组成部分。 平台面向教学、科研与科普活动， 为本地观测实践、天文教育和公众展示提供了新的空间与支撑。

平台概况

源启天文台依托李政道研究所楼顶平台建设，结合屋顶周边空间改造， 旨在建设成为集教学、科研、科普于一体的平台。天文台的建设得到了上海交通大学1989级校友、 云启资本创始合伙人毛丞宇先生的支持。当前天文台已具备开展初步观测和科普活动的条件， 并将随着设备安装和平台完善，进一步拓展其观测与展示能力。

名称寓意

  • “源” 出自校训“饮水思源”，并有“源初”之意。
  • “启” 出自“云启”，兼有“启程”之意。

建设进展

  • 2023 年 1 月：捐建“源启天文台”项目启动。
  • 2023 年 6 月—9 月：方案沟通、审议与公开招投标工作推进。
  • 2023 年 11 月：屋顶改造竣工，天文台开始施工。
  • 2024 年 4 月：项目基建工作完成。
  • 2024 年 12 月：30 cm 口径望远镜完成安装调试，天文台正式启用。

设备与建设规划

  • 已先行安装 30 cm 口径望远镜，用于初步观测与科普活动。
  • 50 cm 大口径望远镜安装工作已进入收尾阶段，预计将很快正式投入使用。
  • 平台后续规划包括望远镜阵列建设，以及与冷湖 JUST-P 的远程联动与合作展示。

与 JUST 的联系

源启天文台与 JUST 系列望远镜在定位上具有互补性。 一方面，源启天文台可承担本地教学、科普和公众活动的展示功能； 另一方面，也可结合观测条件与项目安排，在联合观测、数据共享和公众传播等方面发挥作用。

Yuanqi Observatory

Fig. Yuanqi Observatory

Yuanqi Observatory was formally launched at the Tsung-Dao Lee Institute in December 2024. It is an important part of the rooftop observing platform at TDLI. The platform supports teaching, research, and public outreach, providing a new local space for observational practice, astronomy education, and public engagement.

Overview

Yuanqi Observatory is built on the rooftop platform of TDLI and was developed together with the improvement of the surrounding rooftop space. It is intended to serve as a platform integrating teaching, research, and outreach. The development of Yuanqi Observatory was supported by Mao Chengyu, an alumnus of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a founding partner of Yunqi Capital. The observatory has already begun to support initial observing and public education activities, and its capabilities will continue to expand as further instrumentation and supporting facilities are added.

Name and Meaning

  • “Yuan” comes from the university motto and also carries the sense of origin.
  • “Qi” refers to “Yunqi” and also conveys the meaning of setting out on a journey.

Development Timeline

  • January 2023: the donation-supported Yuanqi Observatory project was initiated.
  • June–September 2023: design consultation, review, and public tendering were carried out.
  • November 2023: rooftop renovation was completed and observatory construction began.
  • April 2024: the main infrastructure work was completed.
  • December 2024: the 30 cm telescope completed installation and commissioning, and the observatory was formally launched.

Instrumentation and Planning

  • A 30 cm telescope has already been installed for initial observing and outreach activities.
  • The installation of the 50 cm telescope is nearing completion, and it is expected to enter formal operation soon.
  • Future planning also includes a telescope array and remote linkage with JUST-P in Lenghu.

Connection with JUST

Yuanqi Observatory and the JUST telescope program are designed to be complementary. Yuanqi can support local teaching, outreach, and public-facing activities, while also contributing to joint observation, data sharing, and science communication when appropriate.
