源启天文台

图：源启天文台

源启天文台于 2024 年 12 月在李政道研究所正式启用， 是研究所楼顶观测平台的重要组成部分。 该平台兼具教学、科研与科普功能， 为本地观测实践、天文教育与公众展示提供了新的平台支撑。

平台概况

依托李政道研究所楼顶平台，源启天文台面向观测实践、教学展示和科普活动等场景。 它既是研究所天文相关工作在本地的重要延伸， 也是连接科研实践与公众认知的一处窗口。

名称寓意

  • “源” 出自校训“饮水思源”，并有“源初”之意。
  • “启” 出自“云启”，兼有“启程”之意。

Yuanqi Observatory

Fig. Yuanqi Observatory

Yuanqi Observatory was formally launched at the Tsung-Dao Lee Institute in December 2024. It is an important part of the rooftop observing platform at TDLI. With functions in teaching, research, and public outreach, it provides local support for observational practice, astronomy education, and public engagement.

Overview

Built on the rooftop platform of TDLI, Yuanqi Observatory is intended for observational practice, teaching display, and outreach activities. It serves both as a local extension of the institute’s astronomy-related work and as a window connecting scientific practice with public understanding.

Name and Meaning

  • “Yuan” echoes the university motto “Gratitude and Responsibility” and also carries the sense of origin.
  • “Qi” refers to “Yunqi” and also conveys the meaning of setting out on a journey.
